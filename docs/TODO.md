 Try These Workflows:

  Workflow 1: Quick Note Capture

  1. Click "Add a Note" button (or Record tab)
  2. Record a test note
  3. Click "Process Recording"
  4. You'll see the new editable review screen with:
    - Editable transcript
    - Contact selector (searchable)
    - People mentioned (add/remove)
    - Key topics (add/remove tags)
  5. Select a contact and click "Save Note"
  6. You'll see a toast notification and redirect to Home

  Workflow 2: Browse Contacts

  1. Click "Look Up Contact Notes" or the Contacts tab
  2. Try the search bar
  3. Click the "Filters" button to see sort/filter options
  4. Click any contact card to see their detail page with timeline

  Workflow 3: Follow-ups

  1. Navigate to Follow-ups tab
  2. See your action items with filters
  3. Click checkboxes to mark tasks complete
